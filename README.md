### Alphabet 2048
*   **Files:** `alphabet-2048/alphabet-2048.c`
*   **Description:** A grid-based puzzle game for sliding and merging tiles.
*   **Usage:** 2048’s tiles are replaced with letters to maintain the shape of a board. Move the tiles around using WASD to represent up, left, down and right, respectively. After each move, a new 2 or 4-tile appears. Score determined by the sum of all the tiles present on the board at the moment. When the board is filled, i.e., no new tile can appear, the game ends. The final score is the sum of all the tiles at the end of the game.
